Welcome To Moon Coffee

Fill your morning with joy and excitement with our coffee of Moon Coffee.

A truly tasty and rich espresso! Perfectly extracted from roasted coffee that is a one-of-a-kind combination of high-quality Robusta and Arabica beans.

At Moon Coffee, an Americano is made by combining espresso with boiling water. You have the option of drinking it hot or cold.

A trendy cup of milk coffee! Our Cappuccino has a little stronger coffee flavor than a Latte, it starts with espresso and then adds milk and foam to make it more appealing. You have the option of drinking it hot or cold.

A cup of coffee with milk is irresistible! The coffee flavor is somewhat milder than Cappuccino. Our Latte starts with espresso, then adds fresh milk and milk foam creatively. You have the option of drinking it hot or cold.

Sweet chocolate sauce, fresh milk, and especially high-quality espresso coffee are mixed together to make a popular drink. You have the option of drinking it hot or cold.

Caramel Macchiato coffee starts with fresh milk and creamy milk froth, which is then blended with rich espresso and delicious caramel sauce. Everything is elevated to art through the skilled hands of the bartenders! You have the option of drinking it hot or cold.

This drink is quite favorite! Premium green tea from Japan is combined with crushed ice, chewy, tasty teajelly, and a coating of cream on top is highly appealing. Freeze Green Tea is tasty and refreshing which conquers anyone!

A delicious drink with the perfect combination of crunchy Oreo cookies with a mixture of fresh milk and condensed milk pureeing with ice cubes, and finally indispensable is the soft whip cream and Oreo cookies puree.

Quality chocolate bars are blended with ice until smooth before being added to the crunchy chocolate jelly and topped with fatty whip cream and sweet chocolate sauce. Creating a delectable Chocolate Freeze that will fascinate anybody!
